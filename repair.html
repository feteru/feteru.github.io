This page is dedicated to repair projects I've done, large and small.

September 2024 - Replacing wahoo screeen

A few months ago, I think my wahoo got some water damage when I was trail riding in a torrential downpour, then left my computer on the bike when we drove home in even worse weather. Since then, there has been a slowly expanding black dot on the screen. After a little while, this got too large to ignore any more, so it's time to replace the screen.

I found this screen on aliexpress

September 2024 - Repairing bottle cages

Somehow over the past couple years I've broken 4 different bottle cages. I took a little while one afternoon to JB weld them all back together. 4 more cages live on for another day. Soon I'll try installing rivnuts on a frame so that I can add more cages.
