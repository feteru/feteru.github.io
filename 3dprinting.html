3D Printing projects

I've done a few projects that are primarily 3d printing. It's super helpful to make one-off stuff quickly and cheaply. I have a Bambu Lab P1 printer, and have been running it for about 18 months now. It's made printing so much faster and easier that it's really fun to take on more complex projects. Something I want to explore more is 3d printed hardware for my sewing projects. It could unlock custom hardware for bags that frees you from some traditional constraints.
